As a Photographer

Began self-taught photography since May, 2021
Currently using a Fujifilm XT-3
Scenery and Humanities

When I was in high school, I was using my father's old Canon 550D on some special school events. It was a quite complex machine for a rookie in photography and I just take photos in auto mode. Later, when I had my first iPhone 7, I found myself interested in photography and maybe somehow talented in it so I started to learn some basic photography knowledge online. And for my last year in undergraduate school, I bought myself a second-hand Sony alpha 6000 with a Sigma 30mm f1.4 and I started to systematically learn photography online.

I found myself not so interested in physical image quality of the photos but the composition, the story inside the photo. Also, somehow because of lack of money, I bought a Fujiflim previous generation flagship camera Fujifilm XT-3 in Black Friday 2021. Fujifilm XT-3 is a APSC frame camera, it's bad at low light enviroment and also no camera stablity when filming. However I like it's color and it's a cost-effective choice for me. Currently, I have a Fujion 35mm F2.0 lens and a Fujion 16-80mm F4.0 lens.
